About Me

Vivek H Sutar is a litterateur, writer, and poet. He has published various literary and non-literary works, with Healing Pages and Echoes of Emotions marking his debut. As the Founder-President of Vatsala Mahila Sanstha, he is devoted to creating meaningful social change.

Born in a small village in Kagal, Vivek excelled academically, topping his 10th board exams at Sanjay Ghodawat Cambridge International School and later studying under the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program. A former national and international karate champion, he is also known for his leadership in student councils and initiatives like AFS programs, MUNs, and international university meets.

His landmark project, HealthForHer, spans 1,227 villages in Kolhapur and aims to eradicate women’s cancer through education, screening, and awareness. With support from NGOs and the district government, and currently in collaboration with Maharashtra's Cabinet Health Minister, he is expanding the initiative state-wide.

Vivek has collaborated with prominent leaders including Sharad Pawar, Nitin Gadkari, Sambhajiraje Chhatrapati, and many others. Inspired by Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj’s legacy, he founded Vatsala Mahila Sanstha to advance gender equity and rural welfare.

He is also active in agriculture, organizing tech events for farmers, and has interned with Shri Dudhganga-Vedganga Sahakari Sakhar Karkhana. Deeply involved in public policy, he works with Raje Shri Samarjeetsinh Ghatge on development initiatives.

Known for his oratory and visionary leadership, Vivek has earned wide recognition for his work in literature and social reform.
