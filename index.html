About Me

Vivek H Sutar is a litterateur, writer, and poet. He has published various literary and non-literary works, with Healing Pages and Echoes of Emotions marking his debut. He is the founder and president of Vatsala Mahila Sanstha, tirelessly working to bring significant positive outcomes to society.

Born and raised in a small village in Kagal, he pursued his education at prestigious institutions such as Shantiniketan and Sanjay Ghodawat Cambridge International School, where he excelled as the topper in his 10th board exams. He continued his high school education with the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program.

Vivek has represented India in national and international karate championships organized by the Shotokan Karate-Do Association of India. During junior college, he served as Vice-President of the Student Council, organizing events like the AFS program, Model United Nations (MUN), and university meets.

His project ‘HealthForHer’ spans 1,227 villages in Kolhapur and potentially benefits over three million women. It raises awareness of women's cancer, promotes prevention, and provides free screening, in collaboration with the district government and NGOs. He is working to expand this initiative across Maharashtra with Cabinet Health Minister Shri Prakash Abitkar.

Vivek has worked alongside leaders such as Sharad Pawar, Nitin Gadkari, Sambhajiraje Chhatrapati, and others. Inspired by Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj’s legacy, Vivek founded Vatsala Mahila Sanstha to extend his impact.

He is active in the agricultural sector, supports farmers with technological initiatives, and has interned at Shri Dudhganga-Vedganga Sahakari Sakhar Karkhana. He also works in public policy with Raje Shri Samarjeetsinh Ghatge.

Known for oratory, vision, and leadership, Vivek has received multiple awards and letters of appreciation for his contributions.
