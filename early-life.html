Vivek H Sutar is a litterateur, writer, and poet. He has published various literary and non-literary works, with Healing Pages and Echoes of Emotions marking his debut...

Since childhood, Vivek has been extremely ambitious and highly decisive, with a competitive spirit. He has represented India in national and international karate championships organized by the Shotokan Karate-Do Association of India...

One of Vivek's remarkable achievements is founding HealthForHer, a project spanning 1,227 villages in the Kolhapur district...

Inspired by his desire to contribute more to society and influenced by his interactions with social leaders, Vivek founded Vatsala Mahila Sanstha...

Dedicated to public policy, Vivek works with Raje Shri Samarjeetsinh Ghatge...
